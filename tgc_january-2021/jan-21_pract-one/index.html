How to make fried rice

Summary

Fried rice is a staple food for many asians
It is usually made with leftover rice from
the previous night's dinner. The ingredients
used can range from as simple as anything in
the kitchen to fancy ones such as abalone
and truffle

Ingredients

  • leftover rice
  • oil
  • garlic
  • shallots
  • eggs
  • mixed vegetables
  • salt

Directions

  1. Heat up the wok on high heat and add in the oil.
  2. Once the oil is hot enough, add in the garlic
    and shallots. Stir it so as to not burn it.
  3. Wait for the fragrance to bloom.
  4. Add in the rice and stir.
  5. When the rice has been coated well with the oil
    and fried, push it aside.
  6. Beat and add in the eggs to the bare wok.
    Slowly scramble the egg to your liking.
  7. Mix the egg and rice.
  8. Season to taste.
  9. Serve the food.
  10. Profit !
